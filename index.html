2019

JANUARY 2024
Federal judge orders unsealing of court documents from Giuffre v. Maxwell civil case. Hundreds of pages released in batches throughout January. Names include Bill Clinton, Prince Andrew, Les Wexner, Alan Dershowitz, and others.

NOVEMBER 19, 2025
Congress passes the Epstein Files Transparency Act (H.R.4405). President Trump signs it the next day. Law requires DOJ to release investigative files within 30 days.

DOJ releases 3+ million additional pages, 2,000+ videos, 180,000+ images. Includes contact lists, flight logs, business records, memos, and photographs. DOJ states this is the final release. Victim advocates criticize: at least 43 victims' full names were accidentally exposed.
